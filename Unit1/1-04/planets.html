Inner Planets

The solar system has 8 Planets
Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune

Mercury is the closet planet to the Sun
It is also the smallest planet in the solar System

Venus is similar in size to Earth, but has a very hot atmosphere.

Earth is the only known planet with life.

Mars is called the "red planet" because its surface is covered with iron oxide.

Jupiter is the largest planet, has a large red spot (storm).

Saturn is known for its beautiful rings.

Uranus rotates "on its side", as if lying.

Neptune is the farthest planet, very cold, has strong winds.
